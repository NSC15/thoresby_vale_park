Cycling Courses

We have six routes designed for all experience levels and ages. There is a selection of bikes to rent (these will require pre-booking before your visit) if you need to do so. We have placed numerous amounts of drink stations and toilets around the park. All facilities will be marked on a map given to you on entrance to the park so please keep this with you. Please see all routes below -

  • Beginner Routes
    • Robin Hood Sprint
    • Sherwood Corner
  • Intermediate Routes
    • Lady Marian Way
    • Miners Climb
  • Extreme Routes
    • Thoresby Colliery Rush
    • Mountain Sprint

Hiking Routes

Explore the 500 acre park through our beautiful hiking routes. Our hiking routes have drinks stations and toilets dotted around so no need to worry. Our team of rangers are consistently driving round the park doing their day to day roles, but fear not they are there to help you if needs be.
We suggest you cater 3 hours of your day... if you wish to see everything the park has to offer.

Glamping Site

We have 15 glamping pods available to book now. All glamping customers get free entry into the park.
All Pods Include -

  • Sleeps up to 4 people
  • Free WIFI (Password left in pod)
  • Complimentary Tea & Coffee
  • 1 Double Bed & 1 Sofa Bed
  • Fresh Bedding & Towels Provided
  • Bathroom with Shower
  • Underfloor Heating for the winter months
